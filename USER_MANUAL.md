## 1. Welcome to Influxity.ai

Welcome! Influxity.ai is a powerful AI-powered platform designed to supercharge your business operations. From generating marketing copy and analyzing sales data to having intelligent conversations, Influxity.ai is your all-in-one solution for business automation.

This manual will guide you through all the features and help you get the most out of the platform.

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1. Logging In

To begin, navigate to the Influxity.ai homepage and click the **Login** button. You will be redirected to our secure authentication provider to log in or create an account.

### 2.2. The Dashboard

Once logged in, you will land on your main **Dashboard**. This is your central hub for accessing all of Influxity.ai's features. From the sidebar, you can navigate to any of the core tools:

-   **AI Chat**
-   **Email Generator**
-   **Sales Copy Generator**
-   **Content Generator**
-   **Data Analysis**

## 3. Core AI Features

### 3.1. AI Chat

The AI Chat feature allows you to have intelligent, real-time conversations with our advanced AI assistant. Use it to brainstorm ideas, ask questions, get advice, or automate simple tasks.

-   **To start a new chat:** Click the "New Conversation" button.
-   **To continue a previous chat:** Select an existing conversation from your chat history.
-   **Usage:** Simply type your message and press Enter. The AI will respond instantly.

### 3.2. Email Generator

Craft the perfect email in seconds. The Email Generator can create professional emails for any situation.

-   **How to use:**
    1.  Select the **type** of email you need (e.g., Sales, Support, Marketing, Follow-up).
    2.  Provide some **context** about the email's purpose (e.g., "Follow up with a new lead from the conference").
    3.  (Optional) Choose a **tone** (e.g., Professional, Friendly, Casual).
    4.  Click **Generate**, and the AI will write a complete email for you.

### 3.3. Sales Copy Generator

Create compelling sales and marketing copy that converts. This tool is perfect for product descriptions, headlines, and calls-to-action.

-   **How to use:**
    1.  Select the **type** of copy you need (e.g., Headline, CTA, Product Description).
    2.  Enter your **product name** or a brief description.
    3.  (Optional) Specify your **target audience** for more tailored copy.
    4.  Click **Generate** to get multiple variations of high-converting copy.

### 3.4. Content Generator

Generate long-form content for your blog, social media, landing pages, and more. This tool helps you overcome writer's block and produce high-quality content at scale.

-   **How to use:**
    1.  Select the **type** of content (e.g., Blog Post, Social Media Calendar, Landing Page Copy).
    2.  Enter the **topic** you want to write about.
    3.  (Optional) Provide additional **details** or keywords to guide the AI.
    4.  Click **Generate** to receive a fully written piece of content.

### 3.5. Data Analysis

Turn raw data into actionable insights. The Data Analysis engine can analyze sales figures, customer behavior, and more to provide you with clear summaries and strategic recommendations.

-   **How to use:**
    1.  Select the **type** of analysis you need (e.g., Sales Analysis, ROI Calculation, Customer Behavior).
    2.  Paste your **data** into the text field. You can use CSV, JSON, or even just a plain text description of your data.
    3.  (Optional) Add any extra **context** to help the AI understand your data.
    4.  Click **Analyze** to receive a report with a summary, key insights, and actionable recommendations.

## 4. Subscription & Billing

### 4.1. Managing Your Plan

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from the **Billing** page in your account settings.

-   **To upgrade:** Choose a new plan and click "Upgrade". You will be taken to our secure Stripe checkout page to complete the process.
-   **To cancel:** Click the "Cancel Subscription" button and follow the prompts.

### 4.2. Viewing Invoices

All your past invoices are available for download from the Billing page.

---

## 5. Account Management

### 5.1. Profile Settings

From your **Profile** page, you can update your name and email address.

### 5.2. Usage Monitoring

Your current API usage for the billing period is displayed on your Dashboard and Profile page. This helps you keep track of your consumption relative to your plan's limits.
